Calcium Carbonate & Dolomite Powder

Keshav Mineral is a manufacturer and supplier of calcium carbonate powder and dolomite powder, serving multiple process‑driven industries. Both minerals are produced using controlled grinding and classification to ensure consistent fineness, purity, and performance.
