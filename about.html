About Us

Our Journey, Vision, and Values

Our Story

Keshav Mineral was established in 2020 by Pranav Maheshwari, a first‑generation entrepreneur who began this manufacturing journey at the age of 21 with a clear and focused vision. Built on the principles of integrity, diligence, and a client‑first approach, the company has steadily served multiple industries by supplying reliable mineral powders tailored to industrial requirements.

As a manufacturer, our strength lies in understanding raw material behaviour, particle size control, purity requirements, and the downstream applications where our products are ultimately used. Every batch manufactured at Keshav Mineral reflects a balance between traditional mineral processing knowledge and disciplined operational practices.

Strategic Location

Located in Alirajpur, Madhya Pradesh, Keshav Mineral benefits from a strategic location that enables efficient servicing of key industrial markets such as Maharashtra, Goa, Hyderabad, and Bangalore.

Our Values

Our work is inspired by the values and guidance of our ancestors, who believed in honest practices and disciplined effort. Their principles continue to guide our business decisions and operations.
