Quality & Manufacturing

Disciplined Processes, Consistent Output

Our Manufacturing Philosophy

At Keshav Mineral, quality is ensured through disciplined processes rather than certifications. All products are manufactured using in‑house grinding, allowing control over fineness and consistency.

Mesh Consistency

Rigorous checks to ensure the particle size distribution meets specific grading standards (100–500 mesh).

Whiteness Control

Standardized selection of raw material to maintain consistent brightness and preventing contamination.

Visual Inspection

Both visual and physical batch-level checks are conducted before dispatch to ensure material integrity.

Logistics

Transport is coordinated by Keshav Mineral to ensure smooth and reliable deliveries to your facility.
