Industries We Serve

Fueling Productivity Across Sectors

We serve process‑driven industries that depend on reliable raw materials.

Paints & Coatings

Used as an extender to provide bulk, improve opacity, and enhance the finish of distempers and emulsions.

Plastics & Polymers

Rubber & PVC

Construction Materials

Essential for wall putty, white cement based mixtures, and other building compounds.

Detergents

Acts as a builder and carrier in detergent powders, aiding in cleaning efficiency and texture.

Ceramics & Glass

Source of calcium oxide in glass melting and flux material in ceramic glazes.
